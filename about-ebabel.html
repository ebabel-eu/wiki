What is eBabel?

eBabel, also known as eBabel Records, is a music label operating in the field of electronic music. It was founded in 2019 by Thomas Amar and David Pitts, in Amsterdam. In 2022, the label activitives moved to Barcelona, and in 2023 to Madrid.

What is the eBabel philosophy?

eBabel is a label that aims to promote electronic music, and to help artists to develop their careers. We are not a big label, and we don't want to be. We want to be a small label, with a small number of artists, and to be able to give them the attention they deserve.

What kind of music does eBabel release?

eBabel releases electronic music, in all its forms. We are not a label that focuses on a specific genre. We release music that we like, and that we think is good. We don't care about genres, we care about music.

How can I contact eBabel?

You can contact us by email, at hello@ebabel.eu

How can I send you my music?

It's best to upload your music to Soundcloud or a similar music sharing website, where only people with your link can listen to it.
